About Stephen
Wardell Stephen Curry II (Akron, Ohio, March 14, 1988) is an American basketball player who belongs to the NBA Golden State Warriors team. At 1.88 meters tall, he plays in the point guard position. He has been a three-time NBA champion and two-time regular season MVP. He is considered one of the best triples of all time, being the one who has scored the most triples in the NBA, and many sports analysts consider him the best 'shooter' in history.
